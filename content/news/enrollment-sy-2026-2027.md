Enrollment for School Year 2026 to 2027 is officially open. The registrar's office welcomes new and returning families Monday to Friday, from 7:30 AM to 11:20 AM and 12:20 PM to 4:00 PM.

Whether your child is joining the Guardians for the first time or returning for another year, our staff will walk you through every step: requirements, ESC voucher questions, and class schedules.

Visit us at Sto. Rosario St., Poblacion, Malinao, Aklan, or reach the registrar at registrar@sjamalinao.edu.ph. We look forward to welcoming your family.
