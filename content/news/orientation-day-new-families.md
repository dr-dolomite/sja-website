To every family joining us this school year: welcome. Orientation day is your chance to meet the faculty, tour the campus, and get your questions answered before classes begin.

Teachers and staff will be on hand to talk through daily schedules, uniforms, and everything your Guardian needs for a confident first day. The session is open to both parents and students.

If you cannot make it, do not worry. The office is glad to walk you through everything on your next visit, or reach us anytime through the contact page.
